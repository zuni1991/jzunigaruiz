C++ was created by Bjarne Stroustrup in the 1980's. He created the language when he was working in his PHD thesis in Cambridge University in England. The first original name was C with classes. In 1985 C++ was implemented commercialy In 1989 it was updated to include protected and static members, as well as inheritance from several classes. Updated again in 2011 to update regular expression support, new loop for syntax, auto keyword, and new container classes.
